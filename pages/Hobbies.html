MOCs

I design LEGO® MOCs over on Rebrickable. I have been building with LEGO since I was little, and have developed a strong passion and understanding of the medium. I mostly build Star Wars MOCs, but I have made a few others from time to time.

My MOCs are designed as display models, as I enjoy getting as much detail into each work as possible. This means that they may sacrifice in stability; however, I always do my best to ensure stability. You can download the instructions for all of my models over on my Rebrickable profile: Profile of Tiggerljc | Rebrickable - Build with LEGO
